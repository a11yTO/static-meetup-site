The Events

The Greater Toronto Area (GTA) is a fantastic hub for digital accessibility and inclusive design. We try to meet once a month for presentations, tech talks, demos and more.

#a11yTO welcomes everyone to our community and our Meetups. Our goal is to provide a place for you to learn, share your experiences, ideas, and solutions and collaborate on building a more inclusive web.

If you're interested in accessibility and inclusive design, this is your home.
