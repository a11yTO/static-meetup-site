The Event

We’re excited to announce a special social event for this month! As we gear up for the upcoming #a11yTO conference, we’re taking a short break from our regular meetups. But we didn’t want to miss the chance to connect with all of you!

Join us on Thursday, September 19th, starting at 6 PM at Firkin on Yonge (207 Yonge St, Toronto, Ontario M5B 2H1) for a casual social gathering. This is a great opportunity to catch up with friends, meet new people, and enjoy some good conversation in a relaxed setting.

Important: The lounge is booked that day for another event, but a11yTO has reserved the back half of the upstairs. We’ll have our own space to connect and enjoy the evening together.

Whether you’re a regular attendee or new to our community, we’d love to see you there! Let’s come together, share our experiences, and keep the momentum going as we look forward to #a11yTO Conf.

We hope you can make it!
