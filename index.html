Meetups

To provide our community with the most accessible experience possible, #a11yTO is not currently using the Meetup platform.

To stay up to date with our events and programming, please visit a11yto.com or subscribe to our newsletter.

The Greater Toronto Area (GTA) is a fantastic hub for digital accessibility and inclusive design. #a11yTO welcomes everyone to our community through our Meetups, and accessibility in general. Our goal is to provide a place for you to learn, share your experiences, ideas, and solutions and collaborate on building a more inclusive web.
