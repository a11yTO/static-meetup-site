The Event

Join us at the League Office for an inspiring panel discussion that delves into the crucial role of accessibility and diversity in driving entrepreneurial innovation. This event brings together distinguished leaders to share their insights, strategies, and success stories on integrating accessibility into business models and fostering inclusive innovation.

What to Expect:

Arrival and Networking (6:00 PM - 6:30 PM):

  • Arrive and network with other attendees in a relaxed atmosphere.

Introduction and Announcements (6:30 PM - 6:35 PM):

  • Opening remarks and important announcements to kick off the event.

Panel Discussion (6:40 PM - 7:25 PM):

  • Engage with our expert panelists as they share their unique perspectives and experiences at the intersection of accessibility, diversity, and entrepreneurship.
  • Learn about innovative business strategies that leverage accessibility to drive growth and success.

Q&A Session (7:25 PM - 7:35 PM):

  • Interactive Q&A session where you can ask questions and gain deeper insights from our esteemed panelists.

Pizza and Networking (7:35 PM - 8:00 PM):

  • Conclude the evening with an opportunity to network with speakers and fellow attendees over pizza. Build connections and discuss ideas in a relaxed and welcoming environment.
