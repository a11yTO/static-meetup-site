The Event

Design tokens are all the rage. They exist to power consistent experiences efficiently. It truly is a powerful thing to deliver platform-agnostic consistent experiences or quickly update a style that propagates via inheritance, even across nested design systems. The resultant consistency and efficiency are the main drivers for their recent popularity.

Design tokens work at the base property level, where the tiniest design decisions are made, for instance, colour, padding, or font weight. How these micro-decisions work together or instead donâ€™t work well together can influence whether the experience is accessible.

Karen Hawkins will show you how design tokens can significantly influence the accessibility of your designs.

  • Karen will show you how design token decisions affect the accessibility of UI elements.
  • You will learn how to use design tokens to improve the accessibility of UI elements.
  • And, you will leave this meetup understanding the importance of using design tokens to ensure a consistent experience across a broad set of UI elements.
