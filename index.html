The Event

Dive into the world of digital accessibility with our upcoming #a11yTO Meetup featuring Lightning Talks. This event is an open platform for anyone interested in sharing their journey and insights into accessibility. Whether you're a seasoned professional or new to the field, we welcome your contributions and stories.

Join us on Wednesday May 15th, from 6:00pm to 8:00pm for an evening of rapid-fire presentations, where speakers will have the floor for short, powerful segments to discuss how they got started in accessibility, share best practices, and recount lessons learned along the way.

What to Expect:

  • Lightning Talks (30-40 minutes): A fast-paced session featuring a series of short talks by diverse voices in the accessibility community, each sharing their unique experiences.
  • Q&A Session: Follow the talks with an open Q&A where you can engage with the speakers and delve deeper into the topics presented.
  • Networking Over Pizza: The event wraps up with an opportunity to network. Connect with speakers and attendees over pizza in a casual and friendly environment.

Why You Should Register:

  • Contribute and Learn: Whether you want to present or listen, this event is a chance to gain and share knowledge.
  • Networking Opportunities: Meet like-minded individuals passionate about improving digital accessibility.
  • Inspiring Stories: Hear firsthand the diverse journeys people have taken in the accessibility field.

Please register to secure your spot as seating is limited. Your registration ensures you a spot at this engaging event, where community, learning, and pizza come together.
