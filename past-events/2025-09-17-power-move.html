#a11yTO Presents:

September 2025 Meetup

Making Digital Accessibility Your Marketing Power Move

  • September 17th, 2025, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • League Offices

The Event

Digital accessibility isn’t just about compliance, it’s a powerful tool for building stronger brands, increasing engagement, and setting yourself apart in the market.

Join us for an insightful session with Matisse Hamel-Nelis, an award-winning Métis communications and digital accessibility consultant, as she shares how integrating accessibility into your marketing and PR strategies can boost your brand’s impact.

This session will explore:

  • The business case for digital accessibility in PR and marketing;
  • How accessible content enhances engagement and audience reach;
  • Practical strategies to make your brand more inclusive;
  • Real-world examples of companies leveraging accessibility as a competitive advantage

About the Speaker:

Matisse Hamel-Nelis is a seasoned communications expert, accessibility advocate, and educator. She is the founder and host of PR & Lattes, a podcast and blog focused on communications, marketing, and accessibility. She also developed and leads the Public Relations Graduate Certificate program at Durham College. Matisse is certified as an Accessible Documents Specialist (ADS) and a Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPACC) through the International Association of Accessibility Professionals.

This is an excellent opportunity to learn how digital accessibility can transform the way your brand communicates, engages, and leads in your industry.
